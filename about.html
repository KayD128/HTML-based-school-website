ABOUT US

Brief History

Precious High School, Araromi, Ilorin, Kwara State was established in 1999 by the Children Evangelism Ministry (CEM). It is a co-educational with a view to fostering and inculcating good and lasting moral values to young ones.

Among other things, the Missionaries who founded this School have a sole aim of instilling genuine discipline and moral uprightness into our educational system in particular and into the polity in general, with a view to turning out leaders who will sanitise our pluralistic Nigeria society and the world at large. Effort is in top gear vigorously to achieve its desired success.

The foundation of the school was laid by the Reverend of a church, His Lordship, Most Rev Dr. Ayo-Maria Atoyebi (OP) on 17th May 1999 and the take-off was on 27th september 1999 with eleven students. To the greater glory of God, the track performance record of the college at WAEC and NECO has been 100% since inception.

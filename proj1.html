Welcome To Precious
High School

The Precious High School is a network of schools established and operated by the Children Evangelism Ministry Int’l (CEM) The CEM is an interdenominational, non sectarian Christian missionary organization committed to the total development and welfare of children.

The Total Child School network forms part of the educational arm of the ministry known as the Sound Education Programme. The Total Child School is a step forward by the ministry in helping to DEVELOP OUR PRECIOUS CHILDREN AS A SOLID FOUNDATION FOR A BETTER TOMORROW’ The Precious High School consists of Nursery, Primary and Secondary School units spread across the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
